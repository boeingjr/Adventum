Ammo for Rumbleshot

Bring Rumbleshot's Ammo to Hegnar Rumbleshot in Dun Morogh.
Rumbleshot's Ammo

Description

Hegnar Rumbleshot sells guns off the road to Anvilmar. Riflemen and mortar teams spend a lot of time practicing out near his shop, and he's always needing fresh ammunition.

But... the last crate of ammo I sent him got lost along the way. My courier said he was camped near the Grizzled Den when wendigo chased him away... and the fool left Rumbleshot's ammo behind!

<name>, can you get that ammo and take it to Rumbleshot? He's been waiting on it and I'm sure he's running low.

Progress

I hope you're not here for ammo, because I'm almost out!

Completion

Great, more ammo!  Old Loslor's shipment finally came through!  The ammo might be late, but just like my grand daddy used to tell me: late's better than never!

Thank you much, <name>.  I already have buyers eager for this stuff!
